Tropical Runners

Racing game with shooter and battle royale elements, where you play as Nina in a deadly race in which traps, firearms, bombs and other mechanical upgrades are made available on the track for runners to use them and win at any cost.

In this game I worked writing code on:
• UI
• Gameplay (racing, cars and power ups mechanics)
