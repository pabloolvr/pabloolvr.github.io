Educa Money

Educational game focused on financial education for children in Elementary School 1 and 2, in which players assume the role of a child who opened a candy store in front of his house, with the goal of accumulating as much money as possible by selling candy, in order to buy toys, clothes, accessories and new furniture for his room.
To achieve this goal, the player must purchase ingredients at the market, cook and deliver the ordered sweets, and return customers' change correctly within a set time limit, while focusing on correctly managing their finances to avoid going bankrupt. The game was developed in Unity for PC and mobile at Umbu Games.

As the main programmer, I was responsible for implementing everything on the game:
• UI
• Drag and drop
• Cooking mechanics
• Market mechanics
• Customer orders generation
• Selling mechanics
• Backend and Save/Load
• Character creation
• Bedroom and character customization
• Tutorial
• Audio
• Difficulty mechanics
