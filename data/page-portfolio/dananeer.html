Dananeer

Dananeer is a tabletop game platform that combines strategic depth with engaging gameplay through its unique modes: Konkan and Okey. Players aim to master the art of melding tiles to outsmart their opponents, employing strategic manoeuvres and adapting to the game's evolving challenges. Developed in Unity for Android and iOS at Blue Gravity Studios

In this game I worked writing code on:
  • • Gameplay Systems using Unity Netcode for Multiplier
    • • Okey Game Mode
    • • Specific Gameplay Mechanics for each Game Mode
    • • AI behaviour
    • • Spectate Mode
  • • Gameplay and Menu UI
  • • Backend integration
