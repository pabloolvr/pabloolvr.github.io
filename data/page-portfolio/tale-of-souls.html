Tale of Souls

Tale of Souls is a roguelike deckbuilder set in a dark fantasy world in which the palyer journey through the randomly generated Duskian Kingdom, build decks with powerful cards, face unique challenges, and navigate a war-torn land filled with mystery and danger. Developed in Unity for PC at Blue Gravity Studios
