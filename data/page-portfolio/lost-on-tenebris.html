Lost on Tenebris

A first-person survival horror in which the player, lost in a spaceship crashed on very dark planet, is an astronaut who must search for an exit while monitoring their scarce light resources — after all, dangers lurk in this terrible extraterrestrial darkness.

The game was developed as a project for the Introduction to Game Development subjet at University of São Paulo, in which I helped in production and gameplay design. Currently I am working on a localization system and game polishing, with fixes in Gameplay and UI, in my spare time.
