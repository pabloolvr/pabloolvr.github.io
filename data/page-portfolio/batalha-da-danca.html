Batalha da Dança

Mobile card battle game with rhythm elements in which cards represent dance moves that gives points to the player, whose objective is to accumulate more points than the opponent at the end of the battle. The game was developed in Unity at Umbu Games.

In this game I worked writing code on:
• All of Gameplay system (except AI)
• UI (Battle scene and parts of main menu)
• Shop and Economy systems
• Inventory and card customization systems
• Save and load system
I was also responsible to manage the publishing of the game in Play Store.
