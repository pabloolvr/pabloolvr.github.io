Batalha da Dança

Card battle game with rhythm elements in which cards represent dance moves that gives points to the player, whose objective is to accumulate more points than the opponent at the end of the battle.

In this game I worked writing code on:
• Gameplay (Everything on the battle scene except AI)
• UI (Battle scene and part of main menu)
• Shop and Economy systems
• Inventory and card customization systems
• Save and load system
I was also responsible to manage the publishing of the game in Play Store.
Game page: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.umbugames.batalhadadanca
